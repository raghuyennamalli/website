Our Interest

The Yennamalli Lab implements computational biology techniques to understand dynamics of molecular machines.

Current work – The Yennamalli Lab works on diverse problems by using computational biology and bioinformatics methods and tools. Some of the current areas of research are:

  • Carbohydrate-Active enZymes (CAZymes) – their molecular function and dynamics
  • Lytic Polysaccharide Monooxygenases (LPMO) – structural analysis and dynamics
  • Comparative genomics of Deinococcus genomes
  • Transglutaminase and their involvement in celiac disease
